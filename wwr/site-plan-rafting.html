Overview

Purpose

The rafting site plan includes the site purpose, the target audience, branding information, and a style guide for the course website project. The website is to help with Visibility and Reach, which allows the company to be more visible to a broader audience beyond their immediate location. It should also help with Booking Convenience, Customer Engagement, and Brand Building.

Audience

Adventure-seeking individuals craving adrenaline-pumping water rafting adventures in the great outdoors, our target audience includes individuals and groups. Whether you're a seasoned rafter or a first-time thrill-seeker, our website caters to those who seek the rush of navigating natural landscapes. Join us for an unforgettable journey filled with excitement.
